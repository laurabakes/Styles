About Styles

Meet the team?

What makes Styles so special?

  • The recipes perfected by Sue in her farmhouse kitchen, using only fresh whole jersey cow’s or sheep’s milk
  • Fresh West Country cream and no artificial colours or ingredients
  • The amazing Styles ‘family’ that work so hard to help the business continue to grow and improve

  • Begining
  • Now
  • Sales
For generations, the Baker family have farmed the land at Styles Farm, on the edge of the beautiful heather moorland, among lush green valleys.

In 1988, as committed foodies, they decided to diversify into one of their favourite foods, luxury ice cream. Without losing the traditional ways of farming on the Exmoor Hills, David and Sue worked hard to perfect their favourite flavours using only the best natural ingredients and creating the most flavoursome ice creams that we have come to know and love as Styles Luxury Farmhouse Ice Cream.

Recognised as the country’s premier producers of homemade farmhouse ice cream, and ‘Slim Ewe’ sheep’s milk Iced Desserts, Styles, that once modestly began with one 1930’s style ‘stop me and buy one’ ice cream trike, has now escalated to a fleet of 9 Ice Cream vans, 3 large parlour units, 4 small ice cream trailers, tent units and a number of trikes.

We attend events throughout the year ranging from village fetes to steam fairs, agricultural shows, regattas, air days and music festivals, as well as selling and delivering our ice-cream to a number of retailers including, cafes, pubs, restaurants and hotels, across the South-West and nationally, by external distributors.

Wholesale

The wholesale side of Styles Farmhouse Ice Cream has grown form strength to strength over the past few years with us not only delivering across Somerset and parts of Devon but also across the South West with the help of external distributors.

If you own your own business or the business you work for are looking for an ice cream provider please do not hesitate to get in touch. We are more than happy to come and see you with samples and further information about what we can offer you and why coming on board with Styles can help expand your business.

We offer a variety of products and services with both scooped or in-house for desserts, our own brand of soft mix, a range of different size cones and the option of standard or luxury, a number of Wall’s popular lollies and of course Cadbury’s Flakes.

Along with this, in order to help advertise the ice cream, whether you’re an existing ice Cream business or whether you’re looking into new options to expand your business, we offer all the point of sale including flavour boards, pavement stands and flags. The flavour boards can be made up to your own specifications and we will do all we can to help ensure the transition is easy and successful for you.

If you would like to arrange for one of our Wholesale representatives to come and see you or if you have any questions at all, please either call the office on 01984 640225 or email them at wholesale@styles-icecream.co.uk

Shows

Nothing at Styles is ever standing still. Across the summer season we are constantly on the move with a number of events of all shapes and sizes, including; village fetes, town competitions, Steam fairs, agricultural shows and music festivals, we like to assume the angle of no job too big, no job too small!

If you would like to find out more about any of our past events or indeed, have a new one that you would like us to be involved with, please do not hesitate to get in touch. We have a number of options available whether it is to arrange for a singular van to attend your event, or whether you would like all 9 of them, two parlours, two trailers and four bikes!

Our luxury ice-cream, high class units and professional staff are sure to be a big hit with the visitors at your event!

We have a very unique small team here at Styles so rest assured your query will be dealt with as soon as possible, with no messing around and definitive answers straight away!
